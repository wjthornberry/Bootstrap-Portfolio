About

Hi, I'm Jake. I'm a full stack web developer in training at Northwestern University's Coding Boot Camp. I have precisely two weeks of class under my belt, but I'm really excited to be a part of this journey. I was born in Palo Alto, raised in equal parts Pennsylvania and Texas, and moved here from NYC two years ago. I work in research administration at Northwestern University, but when I'm not there, studying, or in class, I love to spend my time reading, distance running, and working on photography. I live in Bucktown with my girlfriend, Kathryn, and dog, Harriet.
